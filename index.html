Welcome to My One Page Website

This Website is to inform everyone who eats or cares about what they are putting in their bodies. It shows the nutrition of everyday food. The research is for some of the most important things to add into your body, which are Magnesium, Iron, Potassium, Omega-3, and Vitamin D. Magnesium helps regulate nerves, muscle contractions, protects bone structure, regulates energy production, as well as acidity levels in the stomach. Iron is vital for oxygen transport, muscle function, immune system support, hormone regulation, growth, and development. Potassium helps regulate fluid balance, muscle contractions, nerve signals, and blood pressure. Omega-3 helps support brain, eye development and reduces inflammation. Vitamin D builds and maintains bones.
